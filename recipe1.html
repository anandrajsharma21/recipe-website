Dal Tadka

⏱ Prep: 10 mins 🍳 Cook: 25 mins 📊 Easy 🍽 Serves 4

🧄 Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow lentils (moong dal)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Wash and soak lentils for 15 minutes.
  2. Pressure cook lentils with water, turmeric, and salt for 3 whistles.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add garlic, cook until golden. Add onions and sauté until light brown.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  6. Add red chili powder, stir well.
  7. Pour the cooked dal into the pan and mix. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
