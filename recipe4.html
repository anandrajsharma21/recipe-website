Chicken Biryani

⏱ Prep: 30 mins 🍳 Cook: 45 mins 📊 Hard 🍽 Serves 5

🧄 Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, sliced and fried golden
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Mint leaves and saffron (optional)
  • 4 tbsp oil

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, turmeric, red chili, and biryani masala for 1 hour.
  2. Parboil basmati rice (70% cooked) with whole spices and salt. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a heavy pan. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook on medium flame until done (about 20 minutes).
  5. Layer: spread half the rice over chicken. Add fried onions and mint leaves.
  6. Spread remaining rice on top.
  7. Cover tightly and cook on very low flame for 15–20 minutes (dum cooking).
  8. Gently mix and serve hot with raita and salad.
