Vegetable Pulao

⏱ Prep: 15 mins 🍳 Cook: 20 mins 📊 Easy 🍽 Serves 4

🧄 Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups basmati rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, bay leaves, cloves, and cinnamon.
  3. Add sliced onion and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables and stir fry for 3 minutes.
  5. Add drained rice and mix gently.
  6. Add water and salt. Bring to a boil.
  7. Cover and cook on low flame for 12–15 minutes until rice is cooked.
  8. Fluff with a fork and serve hot with raita.
