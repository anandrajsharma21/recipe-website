About Anpurna

Recipes Made with Love

Welcome to Anpurna — a simple and clean cooking recipes website built with love using only HTML and CSS.

The name "Anpurna" is inspired by Annapurna, the Hindu goddess of food and nourishment. This website brings simple, tasty, and easy-to-follow recipes to your kitchen.

Whether you're a beginner cook or someone looking to try something new, Anpurna has a recipe for you — from everyday dal and parathas to festive biryani and sweet gulab jamun.
