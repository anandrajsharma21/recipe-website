Aloo Paratha

⏱ Prep: 20 mins 🍳 Cook: 20 mins 📊 Easy 🍽 Serves 3

🧄 Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Water to knead dough
  • Salt to taste
  • Filling: 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds, 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp fresh coriander, chopped
  • Ghee or butter for frying

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Knead wheat flour with water and salt into a soft, smooth dough. Rest for 15 minutes.
  2. Mix mashed potato with all filling spices and coriander. Divide into equal portions.
  3. Take a dough ball, roll into a small circle. Place filling in the center.
  4. Seal the edges and gently roll out into a flat paratha.
  5. Heat a tawa (griddle) on medium flame. Place paratha on it.
  6. Cook until brown spots appear, then flip. Apply ghee or butter.
  7. Cook both sides until golden and crisp.
  8. Serve hot with curd, pickle, and butter.
