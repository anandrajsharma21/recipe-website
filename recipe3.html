Gulab Jamun

⏱ Prep: 20 mins 🍳 Cook: 30 mins 📊 Medium 🍽 Serves 6

🧄 Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 3 tbsp milk (to knead)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying
  • For Syrup: 1.5 cups sugar, 1 cup water
  • 4–5 cardamom pods
  • Few drops rose water

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Add milk slowly and knead into a soft, smooth dough. Do not over-knead.
  3. Divide into small equal balls (no cracks on surface).
  4. Heat oil on low-medium flame. Fry balls slowly until golden brown.
  5. For syrup: Boil sugar and water with cardamom for 5 minutes until slightly sticky.
  6. Add rose water and remove from heat.
  7. Drop warm fried balls into the warm syrup. Soak for at least 30 minutes.
  8. Serve warm or at room temperature.
